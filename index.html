How to use this catalog

Technology Platforms

Core facilities and infrastructure available across RARE.Qc member institutions. Browse by domain, type, or institution to find relevant services.

What is a Core Facility?

A shared research facility offering specialized equipment and expertise to multiple research groups, typically with fee-for-service or tiered pricing models.

What is a Data Resource?

A dataset, database, or biobank that researchers can access. May include patient registries, genomic databases, or clinical data repositories.

What is Expertise?

Consultation and knowledge services from specialized researchers or staff—no physical equipment involved. Ideal for methodological guidance or bioinformatics support.

Updating this catalog

This tool reads from JSON data files. To update: edit the Excel sheet, re-run the conversion script, and replace the data files. No code changes needed.
